Vegetable, Black bean and Quinoa Salad

For the salad:
  • 1 cup uncooked red quinoa
  • 1 14 oz can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 red pepper, chopped
  • ¼ cup fresh cilantro, finely chopped
  • 2 green onions
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 avocado, chopped into 1 inch pieces
For the dressing:
  • 4-5 Tbsp. of fresh lime juice (~2 small limes)
  • ½ tsp. Salt
  • ½ tsp. Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • ¼ cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ½ tsp. Ground cumin

  1. Cook quinoa according to the package directions.
  2. While it is cooking, prepare the chopped vegetables and whisk the dressing together.
  3. Allow quinoa to cool about 5 minutes after cooking.
  4. Drizzle dressing over salad and toss with salt and pepper to taste.
