# Pancakes

## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 2 tsp. baking powder
- ¼ tsp. salt
- 2 eggs
- 1 ½ - 2 cups milk
- 1 Pck. vanilla sugar
- Butter for the pan

---

## Method
1. Mix together the dry ingredients.
2. Beat the eggs into the milk.
3. Add wet ingredients to dry.
4. Heat pan to medium/high. Use butter in between each pancake for that nice even color!
