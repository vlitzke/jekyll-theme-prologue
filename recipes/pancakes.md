# Pancakes

## Ingredients
* 2  cups flour
* 2 tsp. baking powder
* Â¼ tsp. salt
* 2 eggs
* 1 1/2 - 2 cups milk
* 1 Pck. vanilla sugar
* Butter for the pan


## Steps  
1. Mix together the dry ingredients.
2. Beat the eggs into the milk.
3. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients.
4. Use butter in between each pancake for that nice even color!
