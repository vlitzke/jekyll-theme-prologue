Vegan Salty Cake (Marc)

Filling
  • Paprika
  • Dried tomatoes
  • Tofu
  • Onions
  • Peanuts
  • Sunflower seed*
  • A bit of olive oil*
*Roasting the peanuts and seeds in the pan before adding the other ingredients is a good idea
Batter
  • 500g flour (I used half wholemeal flour and half normal flour)
  • 50g starch
  • 1 pack of baking soda (10-20g I guess)
  • 10g salt
  • 150g oil (olive is good though expensive)
  • 10g salt
  • 300g soy/rice/oat milk (to adjust depending on the consistence)

  1. Start cooking the filling in a pan, then prepare the batter.
  2. Add the filling to the batter. (The filling should represent 1/3 to 1/2 of the total batter)
  3. Oil a dish and put the batter+filling in.
  4. The filling can be whatever! Just mix some random ingredients. Some crunchy ingredients like nuts is a good idea. A bit of spiciness as well.
  5. Cook at 190Â°C for about 40 min.
